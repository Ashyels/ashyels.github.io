Preface

This book is the work of Ashyels Rosyanna—a composer, author, and app developer. To discover more about Ashyels' creations, artistic endeavors, and personal stories, visit the website at ashyels.com. Stay connected with Ashyels for regular updates on Instagram and YouTube under the handle @ashyels.

Dark Phantasmagoria is Ashyels's third novel project. It chronicles the story of Yukiya Nakahara, a seventeen-year-old who finds himself isolated as part of the minority race in Lanterland. His life takes a dark turn when he is mysteriously cursed by an ancestor to carry out a mission of vengeance—delivering psychological torment to those who have robbed him of his happiness, just as his ancestor once endured.

The Dark Phantasmagoria series emerged during deep self-reflection for the author, drawn from years of personal sorrow. The author channeled these emotions into a powerful narrative through writing, blending horror, thriller, fantasy, and romance elements. Each chapter is imbued with the author's raw feelings, creating a dark, immersive tale.

Set in a meticulously crafted world, the story incorporates familiar global elements—car brands, weapons, and advanced technology—to enhance readers' ability to visualize the scenes. Though fictional, the narrative is enriched by a distinct and recognisable world.

Please note that the story is purely fictional and intended for entertainment purposes. It does not promote or disparage any belief system, race, religion, or cultural practice. The author is mindful of sensitive matters. Additionally, this novel is recommended for readers aged 13 and older, and parental guidance is advised for younger audiences.

Finally, Ashyels extends the heartfelt thanks to all readers for choosing this novel as part of their reading journey. Sending the warmest regards and wishing everyone a pleasant reading experience.
